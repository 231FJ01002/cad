Dr. N. Veeranjaneyulu Professor & HoD, CA Dept.

The Department of Computer Applications at Vignan's Foundation for Science, Technology & Research (Deemed to be University) is Research-oriented, Student-Centered department offering undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Information Technology and Computer Applications. The department promotes world-class research in computer-related disciplines with an absolute commitment to excellence in teaching. The department additionally offers value-addition programs to give extra inputs like communication skills, soft skills and project management skills, etc. The Department is determined to raise the confidence levels of its students and thereby improve their competencies. This finally establishes them as full-fledged professionals for the software industry. The consistent growth and development of a student admitted into the IT program at the institution is reflected in the extraordinary academic results, that is, a pass percentage of 85% and above in examinations, and early placement of a good number of students in IT companies.
